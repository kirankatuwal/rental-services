Our Policies

Vehicle Maintenance and Safety Standards

Our car rental service prioritizes exceptional customer service and satisfaction. To achieve this, we will implement a comprehensive customer feedback system to monitor and address any issues promptly. Customers will have access to 24/7 support through multiple channels, including phone, email, and live chat. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee: if a customer experiences any issues with their rental that are not resolved to their satisfaction, they will be eligible for a discount on their next rental or a partial refund. Our goal is to ensure every customer has a seamless and enjoyable rental experience.

Customer Service and Satisfaction Guarantee

To ensure the highest level of safety and reliability for our customers, all vehicles in the rental fleet must undergo regular maintenance checks. Each vehicle will be inspected and serviced by certified mechanics every 3,000 miles or three months, whichever comes first. This includes oil changes, tire rotations, brake inspections, and other essential maintenance tasks. Additionally, each vehicle must pass a thorough safety inspection before being rented out. Any vehicle failing to meet our stringent safety standards will be removed from the fleet until all necessary repairs are completed.
