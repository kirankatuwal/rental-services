Basic Liability Coverage

The Basic Liability Coverage is included with every rental at no additional cost. This scheme covers damages to third parties' property and bodily injuries resulting from an accident caused by the renter. The coverage limits comply with the minimum legal requirements of the region where the vehicle is rented. This insurance ensures that renters are protected from significant out-of-pocket expenses due to accidents involving third parties, providing peace of mind for both the renter and the rental service.

Comprehensive Protection Plan

The Comprehensive Protection Plan is an optional insurance scheme that offers extensive coverage for the rental vehicle and the renter. This plan includes collision damage waiver (CDW) and theft protection, reducing the renter's financial responsibility for damages to the rental vehicle or loss due to theft. Additionally, it provides coverage for personal accident insurance, covering medical expenses for the renter and passengers in case of an accident. This plan is ideal for renters seeking maximum protection and minimal liability during their rental period.

Premium Roadside Assistance

The Premium Roadside Assistance scheme is designed to provide renters with additional support and convenience during their rental period. This optional insurance covers services such as towing, flat tire changes, battery jump-starts, lockout assistance, and emergency fuel delivery. Available 24/7, this plan ensures that renters can quickly resolve unexpected issues on the road without incurring extra costs or significant delays. The Premium Roadside Assistance scheme offers peace of mind and a hassle-free rental experience, making it a valuable addition for long trips or rentals in unfamiliar areas.
