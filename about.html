About us

Established in 1958 as Thrifty Rent a Car System, Inc., the company has grown steadily over the decades. Thrifty is the brand of choice for deal hunters looking for a great car rental for their next trip, balancing value and adventure to get you back on the road so you can feel like yourself again.

Whether you are travelling with family, friends or your significant other, the team at Thrifty are here to help. Our versatile fleet ticks the box for all kinds of adventures, so if you’re going on a family road trip, skiing with mates, or moving house; we’ve got the car, van or truck to meet your needs.

Thrifty’s free Blue Chip Member Program is designed to benefit savvy shoppers looking to ensure they never miss the best deal (think 15% off every rental and access to exclusive member offers!), plus you’ll hit the road faster by jumping the queue among many other Blue Chip benefits.
